Short biography

I am a Doctor in Philosophy (received my degree at KU Leuven). My PhD thesis was on modal epistemology, and I just completed a postdoc project on know-how.

Area of Specialization: Epistemology.

Areas of Competence: Philosophy of Science, Philosophy of mind, Philosophical Logic, Metaphilosophy, and Metaphysics

Areas of Interest: Modal epistemology, Know-how, Ability, Skill, Understanding, and Models
