I am a philosopher. Originally from Santiago (Chile), I pursued my PhD at KU Leuven's Institute of Philosophy (Belgium), where I also did my MPhil. I mainly work in topics related to the philosophy of modality, in special its epistemology and metaphysics, but I also am competent in general epistemology, philosophy of science, philosophy of logic and philosophical logic, and philosophy of language. I am also interested in a broad range of topics, from ancient skepticism, to the philosophy of mathematics, passing through the philosophy of music and visual arts, Wittgenstein, hypertext and design.

In my spare time, I draw, play guitar and contribute to various open source projects.
