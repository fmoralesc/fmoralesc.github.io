I am a philosopher. Currently I am pursuing a PhD at the Institute of Philosophy at KU Leuven (Belgium), where I also did my MPhil. I mainly work in topics related to the philosophy of modality, in special its epistemology and metaphysics, but I also am competent in general epistemology, philosophy of science, philosophy of logic and philosophical logic, and philosophy of language. I am also interested in a broad range of topics, from ancient skepticism, to the philosophy of mathematics, passing through the philosophy of music and visual arts, Wittgenstein, hypertext and design. Originally from Santiago (Chile), my studies are funded by CONICYT's Beca Chile for Doctoral Studies Abroad.

In my spare time, I draw, play guitar and contribute to various open source projects.
