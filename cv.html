Short biography

I am Doctor in Philosophy (received my degree at KU Leuven). My PhD thesis was on modal epistemology. More specifically, it gives an account of the structure of modal knowledge and of knowledge of so-called agentive modalities and abilities, with the background of knowledge of counterfactuals.

Area of Specialization: Modal Epistemology

Areas of Competence: Epistemology (Formal and Mainstream), Metaphysics, Metaphilosophy, Philosophical Logic, Wittgenstein’s philosophy

Areas of Interest: Aesthetics, Ancient skepticism, Philosophy of Mathematics
